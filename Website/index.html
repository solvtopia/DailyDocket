About The Project

Our Court Docket system is 100% cloud based allowing us to store all your data in a safe and secure data center. Our servers are state of the art and offer the highest performance available along with top-notch security solutions. All access to the system is secured using both hardware and software firewalls and web traffic is made safe using Encrypted SSL Certificates.

Our software automatically processes Court Calendar Updates for both Civil and Criminal cases directly from the state and places them in a user-friendly interface which includes SMS notifications to both you, as the attorney, and your clients.

Use either our web-based interface or our mobile App to manage your client records, stay up to date on when you need to appear in court, and never miss a court date again!
